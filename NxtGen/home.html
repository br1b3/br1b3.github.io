Who is the Nxt Gen?

Well you are, of course!

Nxt Gen is a non-profit Company that provides trainging in Space Science for the next generation of budding young scientists. Nxt Gen's mission is to develop young minds and introduce them into the world of STEM in ways they would not normally get to experience in a school setting.

From the Stars and Beyond

If you are wondering who the Nxt Gen is for then let us clarify. This program is for everyone. Although we strive to aim our cirriculum to young minds we also provide training and interships for budding young adults as well as established adults looking to branch out and diversify their skill set.

One of the best ways to get involed with Nxt Gen is to attend one of our many trianing seminars. During these events you'll be able to learn different aspects of space science and space research. There are events for beginners, mathmeticians, geologists, coders, and more. Be sure to check the Event Calendar for the dates and times of our seminars.

Dip Your Feet Into The Great Unknown

Training is great but have you ever wanted to actually work for NASA? While we are not NASA we do contract with several private and commercial companies involved in space science and research. They are always looking for new talent who are eager to explore the great unknown.
