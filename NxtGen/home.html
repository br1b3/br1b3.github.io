Who is the Nxt Gen?

Well you are, of course!

Nxt Gen is a non-profit Company that provides trainging for young and curious minds. Nxt Gen's mission is to spark an interest in space research and introduce young adults into the world of STEM in ways they would not normally get to experience in a school setting.

From the Stars and Beyond

If you are wondering who theis for everyone Nxt Gen we have exciting news for you. We have offer summer camps and activity nights for small children all the way to research programs and internships for young adults. Our program explores different topics every year an collaborate with professional from the industry. There is no limit to what your child can learn with us.

Help Wanted!!!

If you are looking to be a part of a mission that is out of this world, consider volunteering with us. One of the best ways to get involed with Nxt Gen is check out our Volunteer Center. We are always on the lookout for professionals in the field of mathmetics, geology, coding, and more. You don't have to be a STEM professional to help out either. Activity Directors, Camp Counsolers, and Chaperones are a welcomed with open arms. Every volunteer will need oriation training and we always offer an AMA for ner volunteers looking to offer their time. Be sure to check the Event Calendar for the dates and times of those events.

Dip Your Feet Into The Great Unknown

Training is great but have you ever wanted to actually work for NASA? While we are not NASA we do contract with several private and commercial companies involved in space science and research. They are always looking for new talent who are eager to explore the great unknown.

If you feel up to the task or just looking check out our Event Calendar for a list of our upcoming internships.
