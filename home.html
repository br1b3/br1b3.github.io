A New Evil Hits the Galaxy!

Space Rangers in all sectors are to remain on high alert. A knew being has been spotted in our Galaxy and has already caused mass mayhem. Known by the name Zurg, this mysterious robot/humaniod's motives are currently unknown. There are rumors that Zurg may have some connection to a Space Ranger within our ranks. The speculations have not proven true so far. Even so, we urge all Rangers to stay vigilent. We must protect the Galaxy at all costs!

Making First Contact

It seems like every corner of the Universe is teeming with new life. In our ever changing landscape, how do you begin to build a long lasting friendship with other life forms our there?

That is a good question. In fact, it is a question we have been tackling for Milliniums. Here at Star Command it can feel like you meet a new face everytime you step one parsec in any direction. That is why we urge you to not miss the upcoming seminar in diplomatic relations and best practices when making first contact with aliens from other Star Systems. Special guest speakers will include the Little Green Men and Space Ranger Sox. You'll learn everything from what to do when you meet a new life form to field-tested skills on how to pick up new lanuages quickly. be sure to check the Event Calendar for dates and times.
